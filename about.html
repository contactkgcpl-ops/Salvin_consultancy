Our Story

Built on Trust.
Driven by Expertise.

Salvin Consultants is a premier industrial and business consultancy firm, specializing in helping entrepreneurs and companies start, license, and scale manufacturing and food processing ventures across India.

Founded in 2009 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we started with a simple mission — to make industrial setup accessible, transparent, and hassle-free for every aspiring entrepreneur. Over 15 years, we have grown into a trusted name serving 500+ clients across manufacturing, food processing, pharma, chemical, packaging, and engineering sectors.

With a team of seasoned industry professionals, we bring practical, on-ground expertise to every engagement — ensuring your project is delivered on time, in budget, and fully compliant.
